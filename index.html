OTR (ASC)II

ASCII art is a technique for rendering images, cartoons, and words with the 95 printable ASCII characters.

This is a collection of images from Beyoncé and Jay-Z's OTR II tour, rendered as ASCII art. Hand-crafted by Tara Vancil.
